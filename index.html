Chuu (Kim Jiwoo)

츄 (김 지우)

Chuu (Korean: 츄; Japanese: チュウ) is a South Korean singer and television personality under ATRP. She is a former member of the girl group LOONA and its third sub-unit, yyxy.

Trajectory as a LOONA member

On December 14, 2017, she was revealed as the tenth member of LOONA. Her solo single album, titled "Chuu", was released on December 28. On January 30, 2018, Chuu appeared in Go Won's music video for “One & Only”. On March 30, she also made a cameo in Olivia Hye's music video for “Egoist”. On February 9, Chuu graduated from Hanlim Multi Arts School alongside Kim Lip. On May 30, Chuu debuted as a member of LOONA/yyxy with members Yves, Go Won, and Olivia Hye with the mini album beauty&thebeat. On August 18, Chuu officially debuted as a member of the completed group LOONA. She and her members released the mini album [+ +], featuring the title track “Hi High”.

BBC Lawsuit

On June 3, BlockBerryCreative announced that Chuu would not be taking part in LOONA 1st World Tour: [LOONATHEWORLD] due to schedule conflicts. On July 25, BlockBerryCreative announced that Chuu would not be participating in LOONA's second Japanese album LUMINOUS nor their Japanese promotions. On November 25, BlockBerryCreative released a statement about Chuu’s expulsion and withdrawal from LOONA, stating “there have been reports of abuse of power, including verbal abuse […] the Company has decided to take responsibility by expelling Chuu from LOONA”. However, numerous people from the industry defended Chuu, claiming that she is not what BlockBerry was attempting to portray her to the public. On December 18, 2022, South Korean media outlet Dispatch released a dossier on the subject, including texts alleged to be between Chuu and members of BlockBerryCreative staff, as well as details about her exclusive contract. On February 1, 2023, it was announced that BlockBerryCreative has submitted a petition asking to suspend Chuu’s entertainment activities. The following day, she personally spoke up regarding media reports about BlockBerryCreative’s action against her and LOONA members. On April 7, Chuu became the first artist to sign with the startup company ATRP. On August 4, Chuu revealed that she will release her lightstick named Kkuka. On August 17, Chuu won her lawsuit against BlockBerryCreative. BlockBerryCreative eventually appealed the decision.

Solo Carreer

On August 29, Donga reported that Chuu will be releasing her solo debut album in October. ATRP later confirmed the news. On September 18, a first teaser was revealed, announcing that her comeback mini album is slated for release on October 18. Through a series of teasers, the title was later revealed to be Howl. On February 12, 2024, Chuu announced her fourth digital single, "Chocolate", which will be released the next day. On March 8, the 7th Civil Division of the Seoul High Court ruled in favor of Chuu in the appeal trial of her lawsuit against BlockBerryCreative. On May 22, ATRP announced that Chuu is currently filming a music video in Australia for her next album planned for June. On May 29, ATRP released a series of teaser for Chuu's second mini album Strawberry Rush, scheduled to be released on June 25. On July 19, it was announced that Chuu will be releasing the digital single "Second Love" on July 22.
